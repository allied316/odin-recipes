Beef Short Ribs

This beef short ribs recipe was passed down to me from my mom and grandma. The short ribs are braised for hours in a flavorfull beer and beer broth and turn out delicious and tender everytime.

Ingredients/h2>
  • 1 pound beef short ribs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle stout beer
  • 1 cup beef stock

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Seaon with salt and pepper. Dredge in flour until coated: shake off excess.
  3. Heat olive oil and butter in a dutch oven or large skillet over medium- high heat. Cook ribs until browned, about 5 minutes per sid. Remove ribs and set aside.
  4. Add onion and garlic to the pot: cook and stir until onion is tender, about 5 minutes. Return ribs to the pot; pour in beer while scraing the browned bits of food off the bottom of the pot. Pour in beef stock.
  5. Cover and simmmer over low heat until ribs are very tender, about 2 hours
  6. Serve and enjoy.
